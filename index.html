Groomarts, in collaboration with iPET Network, is thrilled to announce the launch of their comprehensive training courses for professional groomers. The iPET Network Level 3 Diploma in Dog Grooming & Salon Management and Salon Management is designed to elevate the grooming industry by equipping groomers with advanced skills and expertise.

12 OCT 2024

The iPET Network Level 3 Diploma covers a wide range of topics, including breed-specific styling techniques, advanced grooming equipment operation, canine anatomy and physiology, health and safety practices, and effective salon management. The course ensures groomers are equipped with the expertise to provide exceptional grooming services while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare.

12 OCT 2024

The iPET Network Level 3 Diploma in Dog Grooming & Salon Management and Salon Management is now open for enrollment. Grooming professionals, aspiring groomers, and salon owners looking to advance their skills and knowledge can find more information and register for the course via the course bookings page.
